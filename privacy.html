Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

TL;DR

We don't collect anything. Your files never leave your device. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking.

What we don't do

  • No file upload — All Markdown rendering happens in your browser via the File API and marked.js. Your .md files stay on your device.
  • No analytics — We don't use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics.
  • No cookies — We don't set any cookies (except the service worker cache, which is local to your device).
  • No personal data — We don't ask for your name, email, or any personal information.

What Cloudflare might collect

MD Viewer is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., request counts, bandwidth) as part of their infrastructure. This is standard for any website hosted on Cloudflare. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Third-party CDN

We load marked.js from jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net). jsDelivr may log anonymous request data per their Privacy Policy.
