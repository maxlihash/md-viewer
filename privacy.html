Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

TL;DR

We collect nothing. Your files never leave your device. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking.

What we don't do

  • No file upload: all Markdown parsing runs locally in your browser with marked.js. Your .md files stay on your device.
  • No analytics: we don't use any third-party analytics or tracking pixels.
  • No cookies: we don't set any cookies (the only local storage is the Service Worker cache for offline use).
  • No personal data: we don't ask for your name, email, or any account information.

What our host may log

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may log anonymous infrastructure data (e.g. request counts, bandwidth). This is standard for any site hosted on Cloudflare. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Third-party CDN

We load marked.js from jsDelivr (cdn.jsdelivr.net). jsDelivr may log anonymous request data per their privacy policy.
